Frequently Asked Questions

How do I fix a "Missed Punch" for an employee?

Navigate to the employee's timecard, find the day with the "No Punch Out" exception, and manually enter the correct punch time in the "Actual Punches" column. Remember to save your changes.

Can I delegate my approval tasks while I'm on leave?

Yes. Go to the "Delegations" page in your workspace. You can assign another manager to handle your approvals for a specified period.

Where can I see my team's upcoming leave?

The "Teams Leave Calendar" in the "My Team" section provides a visual calendar view of all approved upcoming leave for your direct reports.
