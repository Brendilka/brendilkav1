Brendilka Time Manager

Key Business Processes

Timecard to Payroll Process

1. Roster & Schedule Creation
2. Employee Punch In/Out
3. Timecard Exceptions Flagged
4. Manager Review & Approval
5. Data Sent to Payroll

Employee Onboarding & Data Flow

1. HR Creates Employee in HR System
2. Masterfile Data Imports to T&A System
3. Manager Assigns Pay Rule & Roster
4. Employee is Active for Time Capture

Open Shift Bidding Process

1. Manager Opens Additional Shifts
2. Eligible Employees Notified & Bid
3. System Validates Bids (Rules, Fatigue)
4. Manager Finalizes & Awards Shift
