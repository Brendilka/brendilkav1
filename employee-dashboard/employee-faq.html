Frequently Asked Questions

How do I correct a missed punch?
Navigate to "My Timecard", find the day with the "No Punch Out" exception, and click the "Edit" icon. You can then enter your correct clock-out time for your manager to review.
Where can I see my remaining leave?
You can see a summary on your dashboard or get a detailed breakdown and history by clicking on the "My Leave Balances" tile.
